1. What is skip gram?

There are 2 methods in Word2Vec, and skip gram is one of them.

Basically we looka at a one word in a sequence, and he guess the other ones
("gram" means sequence of words (like anagram))


2. What is other method besides skip gram

CBOW does the exact opposite. It takes the surrounding words and tries to guess the center one
It fits better larger models, so not this one.
